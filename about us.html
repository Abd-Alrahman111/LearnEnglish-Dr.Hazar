Meet Dr. Hazar

Dr. Hazar is a renowned English language educator with over 20 years of experience in teaching, curriculum development, and teacher training. She holds a PhD in English Language Education from the University of Cambridge and has taught English in a variety of settings, including universities, language schools, and online platforms.

Dr. Hazar is passionate about helping students of all ages and backgrounds achieve their language learning goals. She believes that language learning should be an engaging and enjoyable process, and she strives to create a supportive and inclusive learning environment for her students.

Dr. Hazar's Certificates and Achievements

  • Certificate in Teaching English as a Foreign Language (TEFL)
  • Certificate in Online Course Design and Delivery
  • Winner of the 2020 International Teaching Excellence Award
  • Author of "Effective English Language Teaching: A Practical Guide" (published by Cambridge University Press)
  • Presenter at the 2019 International Conference on English Language Teaching
  • Developed curriculum for English language programs at major universities in the United States and United Kingdom
  • Consultant for major publishing companies on English language education materials

Dr. Hazar's dedication to excellence in teaching has been recognized by her colleagues and students alike. She has received numerous awards and accolades for her innovative teaching methods and her contributions to the field of English language education.

The Learn English Website

The Learn English website is an online platform designed to help students of all levels improve their English language skills. The website offers a variety of courses, including grammar, vocabulary, pronunciation, and conversation practice, as well as test preparation courses for exams such as TOEFL and IELTS.

Dr. Hazar has personally developed many of the courses offered on the website, and she works closely with a team of experienced teachers to ensure that the courses are engaging, effective, and up-to-date with the latest developments in English language education.

The Learn English website also features a range of resources to support language learning, including interactive exercises, quizzes, and videos. Students can track their progress through the courses and receive personalized feedback and support from experienced teachers.

Whether you are a beginner or an advanced learner, the Learn English website offers a range of resources and courses to help you achieve your language learning goals. Join our community of learners today and start your path to fluency with Dr. Hazar and her team!
